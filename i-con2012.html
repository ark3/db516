I-CON 31
March 30–April 1, 2012


Docking Bay 516 at I-CON 31

DB516 is proud to announce that we will once again be making our annual appearance at I-CON, Long Island's own science fiction convention! We are back with a greatly expanded set of programming covering a wide variety of fandom topics.

As Long Island's Star Wars fan club we will of course be discussing all things Star Wars. Join us at our Star Wars Prequel Trilogy panel for a discussion of Palpatine's plots and Anakin and Obi-Wan's adventures, as well as the recent re-release of The Phantom Menace in 3-D. If classic Star Wars is more your style, our Star Wars Original Trilogy panel will be just what you are looking for. We'll be discussing the adventures of Luke, Leia, Han, and Chewbacca that started it all, as well as the changes that have been made to the trilogy over the years. If that's not enough for you we will also be running both a Star Wars Expanded Universe panel and a Star Wars: The Clone Wars panel. These will be looking at the books, comics, and video games that have expanded the Star Wars universe, and the popular series currently on Cartoon Network.

But we're not just a Star Wars club! Our programming at I-CON covers a wide variety of other fandoms! From James Bond to Star Trek and Harry Potter to SouthPark, we'll have something for fans of almost every subject!

Fans of science fiction movies and television can join us for our 2011 - 2012 Movie Roundup, a look back at the sci-fi, fantasy, and superhero movies of the past year. In the Movies of 2012 Trailer Park we will be looking ahead to this year's most anticipated blockbusters. We will also be talking about which Hollywood Reboots and Sequels have worked well, and which ones really didn't. But don't think it's all about the movies. Not to be forgotten is sci-fi on the small screen. Join us for our discussion of the current state of Science Fiction on Television. Which shows are worth watching? And why do so many keep getting canceled? In addition to this overview many popular programs will have their own discussions as well, including Warehouse 13, Firefly, Stargate, The Vampire Diaries, and The Big Bang Theory.

Doctor Who and Torchwood will both have a huge presence at I-CON this year. Docking Bay 516 will be co-hosting several discussions about the popular British show and its spin-off. From Rose to Amy and Rory we will be discussing the Companions of New Doctor Who. We will also be trying to unravel the complicated timeline of River Song. Now that we know most of her story, does it even make sense? Speaking of complicated timelines, we will also be Counting Jacks. Just how many Captain Jacks are sneaking around in Cardiff at any given time? You can also chime in with your opinion on current show-runner Steven Moffat at our panel, In Moffat We Trust(?). Classic Who fans shouldn't worry though. It's not all New Who. We'll also be looking at some Doctor Who's Greatest Classic Series Moments.

Fans of science fiction and fantasy novels should check our new panel, The Book Was Better (Or Was It?), a discussion of the many sci-fi, fantasy, and young adult novels that Hollywood has turned into successful TV shows and movie franchises. New for I-CON this year are our discussions on the popular teen series The Hunger Games, and the hit HBO series Game of Thrones. And returning to I-CON is our popular Lord of the Rings discussion, which has been newly expanded to include the upcoming film adaptation of The Hobbit.

Last but not least, make sure to check out Docking Bay 516's signature programming, our ever-popular game shows! All your old favorites are returning, including Star Wars Movie Quotes Trivia, Wheel of Fandom, Sci-fi/Fantasy Pyramid, and Fandom Feud! And if that isn't enough, we'll also be introducing a brand-new game show for I-CON this year, Name That Sci-Fi Tune!

All this and much more will be waiting for you at I-CON 31! We'd love to see you there!

Friday

Stepping through the Stargate — SAC 308 6 PM
Join us for a trip through the Stargate. Discuss the fan favorite Stargate SG-1, as well as its two spin-off shows: Stargate Atlantis and Stargate Universe.

Star Wars Movie Quotes Trivia — SAC 306 7 PM
Test your knowledge of the six Star Wars movies and compete for prizes. You'll remember this popular panel from past I-CONs, but this year we've spiced it up to make it faster and more intense!

South Park Character Creation Workshop — SAC 308 7 PM
Kick it old school! Make your own 2 foot construction paper characters for the town of South Park the way Trey and Matt originally had done in their classic animations. Audience participation a must!

Sci-Fi/Fantasy Pyramid Game — SAC 306 8 PM
Remember $100,000 Pyramid? Well here is the I-CON version, with fun prizes instead of the $100,000. Try to guess the words or phrases described by your partner as the clock ticks down.

South Park Trivia — ESS 183 8 PM
How much do you know about South Park? Join the fun.

Science Fiction on Television — SAC 308 8 PM
Discuss the current crop of network and cable genre offerings, including Terra Nova, Falling Skies, The Walking Dead, Fringe, and much more.

Vampire Diaries — SAC 306 9 PM
Tired of vampires that sparkle? Bored with the current popular versions of vampires and werewolves? The Vampire Diaries is the CW's hit show featuring the supernatural forces of vampires, werewolves, witches and all kinds of mayhem. Let's explore the many sides of this show and see what makes it the new standard in supernatural shows.

Computer Animated Movies — SAC 306 10 PM
We take a look at movies and television shows with visuals done entirely in computer-generated graphics, like Toy Story, Shrek, or Star Wars: The Clone Wars. What types of stories work well in this genre? How realistic can the rendering become? Who are the innovators in this genre?

Hunger Games — SAC 309 10 PM
The popular young adult series has led to a movie currently in theaters. Discuss the choices made by the characters, the way of life in the districts and the capital, and much more. May the odds be ever in your favor!

Saturday

Costumes, Characters, and Cameras — SAC 306 10 AM
A workshop for costumers and photographers to learn to make better pictures together. Costumes and cameras are encouraged. There will be interactive audience participation.

Star Wars Prequel Trilogy — SAC 308 10 AM
Discuss the Prequel trilogy, from Palpatine's plots to Obi-Wan & Anakin's adventures. Newly revised and updated for The Phantom Menace 3D.

Big Bang Theory Discussion — SAC 306 11 AM
Dive into the world of Sheldon, Leonard, Penny, and the rest as we discuss this popular, award-winning comedy that seems to have polarized viewers all over.

Miku Miku Dissection — SAC 309 11 AM
Discussion of popular Vocaloid songs with ambiguous meanings.

Fandom Feud — SAC 306 12 PM
The most popular DB516 game show is back! Teams compete for prizes by guessing popular answers to survey questions about your favorite areas of fandom.

Wheel of Fandom — SAC 306 1 PM
You've seen it on TV. You know how it works. You just can't wait to spin that wheel, guess the puzzle, and win prizes. Another popular DB516 game show returns.

Companions of the New Doctor Who — SAC 306 2 PM
Oh My God, I'm the Tin Dog: Companions of the New Doctor Who (2005 to Present). A lively discussion of the strengths and weaknesses of the TARDIS travelers of the 21st Century, from Rose Tyler to the Ponds.

Trailer Park: Movie Trailers — SAC 306 3 PM
Watch previews for the upcoming movies of summer 2012 and beyond. This year will feature The Avengers, The Dark Knight Rises, Prometheus, The Hobbit, and much more.

Warehouse 13 — SAC 308 4 PM
Snag it, bag it, and tag it! Let's talk about the great characters and awesome artifacts on this light-hearted, fun, and popular Syfy TV show. What are your favorite artifacts and guest stars?

Superheros in Movies and Television — SAC 306 5 PM
Comic book superheroes are more popular than ever in television and movies, including live-action blockbusters and children's cartoons.

Torchwood: Counting Jacks — SAC 308 5 PM
A look at Captain Jack's complicated timeline, with a focus on the time of the Doctor Who episode "Boomtown." Just how many Jacks are there in Cardiff at that time? You may be surprised.

Star Trek: Where is it going? — SAC 308 6 PM
With no television shows currently on the air, and the success of the recent reboot movie, where is Roddenberry's sci-fi franchise headed today?

Game of Thrones — SAC 306 6 PM
"You know nothing, Jon Snow!" Discuss your favorite scenes, characters, and story arcs from the hit HBO show, as well as what you're expecting from the second season. Discussion of the books is welcome, but try not to spoil anything for fans of the show.

Bond Girls Exposed — SAC 306 7 PM
Celebrating 50 years of the women of 007. Highlights and analysis of the evolution of a key component to the success of the longest running film franchise in history.

The Book Was Better, Or Was It? — SAC 308 8 PM
Discuss how Hollywood has turned science fiction, fantasy, and young adult novels into popular movie and television franchises. Compare the books to recent screen renditions such as Harry Potter and Game of Thrones, as well as classics like Dune and War of the Worlds.

Sunday

Firefly — SAC 306 10 AM
We aim to misbehave! Join us for a discussion of Joss Whedon's Firefly and Serenity. Talk about the characters, episodes, and movie, as well as the Browncoat fan phenomenon that has developed around the short-lived series.

Star Wars Original Trilogy — SAC 308 10 AM
Take a look at the adventures of Luke, Leia, Han, and Chewbacca that started it all. Discuss your favorite characters and scenes, as well as the tweaks and changes Lucas has made to the films over the years.

Hollywood Remakes and Sequels — SAC 309 11 AM
Discuss the apparent lack of creativity from Hollywood lately. There have been many reboots of popular franchises as well as a few surprise sequels. Which ones worked or didn't work? Why can't they leave well enough alone?

James Bond Trivia Contest — SAC 306 11 AM
From Oddjob to Skyfall, test your knowledge of all things Bondian, and win a prize!

Photoshop: Pushing Pixels to Improve Images — SAC 306 12 PM
Pushing Pixels to Improve Images and Make Masterpieces. Photoshop is a powerful, complex program made of lots of little things. Don't feel overwhelmed, let's take it one piece at at time.

Minecraft — SAC 309 12 PM
Discussion and creative play with blocks.

Doctor Who: In Moffat We Trust? — SAC 308 1 PM
Highlights and analysis of the impact that show runner Steven Moffat has had in the hearts and minds of fans of a show which is now at its zenith of popularity while remaining controversial to many fans.

Name That Tune — SAC 306 1 PM
Name that tune you have heard in Sci-Fi/Fantasy movies and TV. Compete for prizes while you meet fellow movie and TV theme enthusiasts.

Harry Potter Discussion — SAC 309 2 PM
General discussion covering many hotly-debated topics from the most successful young adult fiction franchise in recent memory. Topics include character motivations, artifacts and MacGuffins, house interplay, and much more.

Star Wars: The Clone Wars — SAC 306 3 PM
Discuss the animated Star Wars TV series airing on Cartoon Network. We will cover a broad array of topics, including the characters we love and hate, the types of stories that work well or poorly, and the pros and cons of the computer animated format.

Harry Potter Trivia — SAC 306 4 PM
Test your knowledge of the wizarding world! Wizards, muggles, squibs, and all the rest are welcome to try and win fun prizes.

Movie Roundup — SAC 308 4 PM
We'll be taking a look back at all the sci-fi, fantasy, and comic book movies that have been released since last I-CON. What did you love, what did you hate, and were there any surprises in theaters this past year?

Star Wars Expanded Universe — SAC 308 5 PM
A look at the books, comics, TV shows, and video games expanding the Star Wars universe, including the popular new online game, Star Wars: The Old Republic.

Lord of the Rings — SAC 306 5 PM
Gondor calls for aid! Join us for a humorous discussion of JRR Tolkien's iconic fantasy series with a focus on the popular Peter Jackson movies and the upcoming film adaptation of The Hobbit.
