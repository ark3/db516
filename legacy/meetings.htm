Meeting Information
Meetings are usually held the second weekend of the month, in the Broadway Mall food court.  You can find us in the back corner by the elevator.  During the summer months (weather permitting), meetings are combined with picnic/bbq's at Cantiague Park (see below).  11AM to Noon is official business, followed by general discussion. 
New people are always welcome!
For other upcoming events, visit the forum.

Cantiague Park

Cantiague park is located right near Broadway Mall.  Coming from the north (LIE and NS parkway), go down 106/107 to W. John Street (just south of the mall).  Make a right, and stay on W. John until you come to the park on your right. 

DB516 meets in the picnic area, located north of the playground (past the restrooms).
