DB516 Event Attendance Policy

Last updated January 2005

 
DB516 is a fan club for all ages. In order to help members enjoy our events safely and to protect the club from any unfortunate incidents, we have set forth these policies. We are taking special care to protect our younger members. Parents are encouraged to get involved.

Members under age 13 must be accompanied by a legal guardian over age 18. The guardian must be present at the event at all times.

Members under age 18 must present a signed permission slip for a given event in order to participate without a guardian present.

Alcoholic beverages may be present at some club events. While no alcohol will ever be served to a minor by the group, events sometimes take place in venues where alcohol is served. Adult members of legal age may be drinking alcohol during such events. Any event that may include alcohol will be clearly described as such in advance.

If you have any questions, concerns, or comments about this policy or about DB516, please do not hesitate to contact us. You can reach us by email: president@db516.com.
