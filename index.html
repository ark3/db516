Welcome to DB516

Docking Bay 516 is the Long Island chapter of Fan Force, a worldwide collection of local Star Wars fan groups connected with The Force.Net.

Upcoming Meetings

At the end of August, our usual meeting location, Game Master Games, will be shutting down. We are transitioning to social meetings during pub or dinner outings. Until things settle down into a routine once again, please check our Facebook page for information about upcoming meeting dates and locations.

In the past, meetings were generally held on the second Sunday of every month starting at 11 AM, with discussion of club business starting promptly at noon. The club usually met at Game Master Games in Hicksville near the back. When the weather is pleasant, Cantiague Park provides a lovely outdoor alternative (see also the old meetings page with its nifty map of Cantiague Park). In the distant past, we used to hold meetings at the Broadway Mall food court near the elevator.

Future meeting dates and locations to be determined. Please see our Facebook page for the latest information.
